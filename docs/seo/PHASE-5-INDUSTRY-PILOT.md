# Phase 5 industry-specific pilot

Implemented 23 July 2026.

## Scope

This pilot contains exactly six industry pages:

1. HMRC enquiries for landlords
2. HMRC compliance checks for construction and CIS businesses
3. HMRC enquiries for restaurants and hospitality businesses
4. HMRC undeclared-income questions for online sellers
5. HMRC enquiries for taxi, private-hire and delivery drivers
6. HMRC enquiries for doctors, dentists and healthcare professionals

No location, software, language or severity variants are included.

## Publication standard

Every page contains original industry-specific coverage of:

- income sources;
- records and operational systems;
- expense risks;
- VAT, PAYE, CIS or other relevant taxes;
- likely HMRC questions;
- an evidence-led practical example;
- official sources;
- a related problem, focused question and canonical guide;
- a category-and-subcategory preselected enquiry CTA.

## Measurement gate

Measure each URL separately in Search Console for impressions, query relevance, average position and click-through rate. Compare enquiry starts and completed submissions by the preselected category and subcategory. Do not expand the pilot until the pages generate qualified industry-specific impressions or enquiries and the query-overlap review confirms they are not competing with their canonical guides.
